WE NEED YOUR HELP

There are many steps you can take to advocate for the environment. Here are some ideas!

Steps You Can Take

1. Petition

Although you may feel like one small signature on a website won't make a change, petitions are a great way to show the government and leaders our opinions on their decisions. Our voice matters and petitioning is a great way to use it.

2. Call The White House

This one might seem a little scary at first, but this can make just as big a difference as signing a petition. Using direct communication is an effective and efficient way to get your point across to authorative figures. Phone messages are quick to record and can be passed down directly to the president.

3. Raise Awareness

Raising awareness is self explanatory - do your own part by helping and educating your friends and family about the Willow Project. Share advocation posts on various social media platforms to bring attention to this issue. We actually discovered this issue through social media ourselves - news spreads around quickly!
